About Us

Netrajyoti Optics

The Netrajyoti Optics Shop is dedicated to providing you with the highest standards of professional eye care, together with a friendly service, quality products and excellent value.

The Optics shop provides a comprehensive eye care service. This is reflected in the range of spectacle frames on offer. Our aim is to cater for all spectacle wearers, irrespective of age, budget or requirements.
